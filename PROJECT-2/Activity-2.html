Michael Jordan

, also known by his initials MJ, is a retired American professional basketball player. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time. Jordan played 15 seasons,in the National Basketball Association (NBA) for the Chicago Bulls and Washington Wizards. He was known for his scoring ability, athleticism, and competitive spirit.When he was 10 years old, he was cut from his high school basketball team, which motivated him to work harder and improve his skills. Jordan went on to win six NBA championships with the Chicago Bulls and was named the NBA Finals MVP in all six of those championships. He also won two Olympic gold medals, one in 1984 and another in 1992as part of the Dream Team After retiring from basketball, Jordan became a successful businessman and owner of the Charlotte Hornets.
